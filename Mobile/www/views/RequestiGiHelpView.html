Know they know

An iGi is short for "I got it!", and is how you "know they know." When you request an iGi, your followers will be prompted to give an iGi when they receive your message, which we receive and make instantly available to you in real-time with this app.

Important Note: Responses received via SMS (text), including iGi's, may not be correctly matched to your broadcast, as there's no way to be completely sure which broadcast is being responded to. For high iGi accuracy, encourage your followers to receive your messages, and give their iGi's or replies, via this app or email.
