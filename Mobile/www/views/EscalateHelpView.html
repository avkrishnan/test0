Escalate ?

An "Escalated" message may repeat or try different methods, if the first message isn't responded to. It may first try an email, then try SMS (text) or the app; or it may start with the app, repeat the attempt again later, then try SMS (text).

The number of attempts and the methods used are determined by how long you'd like us to escalate your message, and how aggressively. Escalation will continue for each follower until a response is received or until the time specified expires.

CAUTION: Escalation is quite powerful; only use above the lowest aggressiveness when absolutely necessary, or you might frustrate your followers.
