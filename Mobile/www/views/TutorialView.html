Introduction to Evernym Channels

Notifying your group can be hard Evernym Channels are simple!
Create a channel, then add or invite others to "follow" it No more gathering or maintaining lists of contact info
Everyone gets messages how they want (email, text, app) Urgent messages can "escalate" (repeat, try other devices) You'll know who got it, and who didn't
Even if only for emergencies, every group you're part of should have its own Evernym channel
Now notifying your group is simple, and quick. And it's free!
