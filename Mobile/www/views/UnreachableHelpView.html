Un-reachable Followers help ?

An un-reachable follower has no verified email address or phone number that we can use to deliver a message.

A follower becomes un-reachable if all of their methods of communication are deleted, or if it's replaced but hasn't been verified yet.

If the follower has an evernym account (little 'e' in the lower right of their avatar), then he/she can edit their own information. If the follower is still a "guest," then you (or someone you authorize) can make any edits.
